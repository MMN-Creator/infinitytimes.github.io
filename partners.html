Our Partners

TETA HUGUETTE BERENICE

Teta has been a valued partner during the last two festive seasons, collaborating on a fashion show and a clothing line for children in the GLM Association. Their partnership continues to flourish.

IGANZE ORIA MILKA

Iganze serves as the official makeup artist for IT models, providing her cosmetics for every fashion show. Their ongoing partnership also includes promotional photoshoots for her brand, AKILA.

URIELLE RUBAYITA

Urielle Rubayita brings her unique expertise in fashion, where she played a pivotal role in the recent denim collection, drawing inspiration from her extensive research on sustainable fashion. Her guidance has been valuable, and her collaboration with IT continues to shape the brand’s vision for eco-conscious creativity.

MACKENZIES by ZOI

Infinity Times partnered with The Mackenzies to launch the Ideal-Gala Dress fashion show, a landmark event that generated over five million dollars in sales. This partnership is ongoing, with both brands continuing to collaborate on these high-demand dresses.

HER MAJESTY COSMETICS

Infinity Times also collaborates with Her Majesty Cosmetics for a range of exclusive photoshoots featuring Infinity Times products. Notably, Her Majesty Cosmetics served as the official makeup artist for the Wedding Dress Dress-Down Show, further solidifying a strong and mutually inspiring partnership.

MISS UNIVERSE

Infinity Times was honored to be the official gown designer for the 77th Miss Universe, outfitting all the contestants in exclusive, elegant creations. This partnership is set to continue, with Infinity Times committed to creating gowns for future Miss Universe events.

PRESENCE by ntWali

Infinity Times collaborated with the luxury fragrance brand Presence by Ntwali. IT models participated in the photoshoot for the fragrance’s launch and continue to represent the brand in ongoing advertisements, blending fashion with the art of scent.

ECO-THREADS

Infinity Times proudly partners with Eco-Threads, which supplies sustainable, high-quality threads for IT designs. Together, both brands are dedicated to promoting eco-friendly, sustainable fashion that makes a positive impact.
